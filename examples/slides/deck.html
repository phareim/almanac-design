— Part I

Five rules of the room

— The Five

What the page asks of you

  • — Hairline, not box.
  • — Accent only at attention.
  • — Serif body always.
  • — Stars dark-mode only, sparse, static.
  • — No chrome.
— Why

Restraint earns the eye

A page that asks for attention everywhere is a page that gets none. The almanac form puts one mark per page, one accent per moment, and one rule between sections. The reader feels the calm before they notice the choices that produced it.
